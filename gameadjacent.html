What Can I Do With This Major?

Game Adjacent            Games Industry Positions

Area

Film, TV, & Media (Entertainment)

With the inclusion of game engines like Unreal Engine and Unity into the television and film industries, those industries are seeking graduates with expertise in game engine programming, lighting, modeling, optimization and more.

  • Game Engine Generalist (Entry-Level Jobs) (Internships)

  • Technical Artist (Entry-Level Jobs) (Internships)

Area

Architecture

Game Design students often emerge with visualization skills relevant to the architectural field. Graduates can work in the architectural and planning industries using game engines like Unreal Engine, or 3D modeling software like 3ds Max and Blender, and applying their understanding of lighting and post-processing to architectural applications.

  • UE4 / Unity Positions (Entry-Level Jobs) (Internships)

  • Visualization (Entry-Level Jobs) (Internships)

Area

Concept Art

Many industries besides games require concept artists. From signage and display fabrication companies to event-vehical design/fabrication companies. Game design graduates are very familiar with the iterative nature of presenting numerous concepts and iterating various designs and styles, which makes them good for working as concept artists in many other fields.

  • Concept Artist (Entry-Level Jobs) (Internships)
